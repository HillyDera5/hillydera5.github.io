STAR STRUCK

This is a cutting edge bluetooth keychain app designed to revolutionize how you manage your keys and valuables With STAR STRUCK, you can say goodbye to the frustration of misplacing your keys. The app utilizes Bluetooth technology to connect your compatible keychain devices, allowing you to easily track and locate your keys with just a simple tap on your smartphone screen.

INFINITI

INFINITI is an online payment app for money transfers, both local and abroad. You can send money to family members living outside Nigeria with ease. You can track your expenses, finances, set budgets, and recieve real-time notifications for your account activity, giving you full visibility and control over your finances. The app's intuitive interface and smooth navigation makes it easy for users of all levels to utilize its features effectively.
